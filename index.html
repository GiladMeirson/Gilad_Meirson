Project: To build my first AI

This project is one of my favorites.
The rules are very simple.

1) Each dot represents a bacterium/human or any living creature by color and number.
2) Every creature has a brain that initially works randomly, it can turn up, down, left, right or stop, the speed he moves depends on the genes he received.
3) Each generation gets 10 seconds to live, each second is 10 steps of the creature, which means each creature gets 100 options for steps.
4) The red zone is dangerous for living beings like dangerous radiation, if they finish the 10 seconds and they are in the red zone they will die, if they managed to escape from there they will have children.
5) During reproduction, the genes are replicated with a certain mutation rate for the following children. Each child is marked with his father's serial number + 100
For example: 104 his child is 204 and his child is 304.
Instructions:
*press start then wait 10 sec.
*press kill to kill the outsiders.
*press reproduce to replicate the best.
